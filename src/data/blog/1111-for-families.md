If you haven't heard of `...` (or commonly known as  dot) before, it is a fast and secure DNS resolver provided by *Cloudflare,* and its free of cost. Its popularity has grown so much so that it has become the second largest DNS resolver after Google.
Since they have become very popular, on st April , they have launched  dot for families. The primary objective of their new DNS resolvers are to filter out bad sites and to keep adult content from being accessed by kids. Their two new offerings are `...` (malware) and `...` (malware and adult content). 

If you're using IPv, then you can configure using these settings:

- Malware Blocking Only
    - Primary DNS: `...`
    - Secondary DNS: `...`
- Malware and Adult Content
    - Primary DNS: `...`
    - Secondary DNS: `...`
